    Magic Thesis

    • Magic is remembered.
    • Magic is personal.
    • Magic is living.
    • Magic is limited by control.
    • Rift Magic is the system failure state.

    Primary Pillars

    • Affinity: natural, conceptual, hybrid, or Rift-touched alignment.
    • Dominion: inner landscape and private source.
    • Manifested Tool: soulbound object or weapon.

    Affinity Families

    • Elemental: fire, water, earth, wind, nature.
    • Energy: lightning, gravity, kinetic, plasma.

    • Dimensional: time, space, Rift.
    • Construct: summoning, runes, golems, binding.
    • Rift-Corrupted: Void Flame, Abyss Tide, False Reflections.

    Casting Methods

    • Conduits: wands, staves, rings, relics.
    • Gesture casting: combat casting, flow weaving, dance magic.
    • Incantation and runes: verbal spells, sigils, arrays.
    • Freeflow: instinct, emotion, Void shaping.
    • Hybrid casting: personal style, risk, innovation.

    Forbidden Magic

    • Consumes caster: blood magic, fleshcrafting, bone sigils, echocasting.
    • Warps soul: soul leeching, shadebinding, mindwalking.
    • Defies reality: temporal rewriting, Rift summoning, name weaving, unmaking ritual.

    Inner World Systems

    Dominion, Guardians, Tools

    Dominion explains why the same Affinity behaves differently across characters.

    Identity -> Terrain -> Pact -> Weapon

    Dominion

    Personal pocket dimension, soul landscape, Affinity amplifier, memory vault, and emotional mirror.

    • Power source: increases endurance and spell scale.
    • Personal battlefield: alters terrain, physics, and entry conditions.
    • Higher understanding: reveals ancestral secrets or forbidden Affinity.
    • Risk: collapse, overextension, Rift erosion, Hollowed state, or Dominion theft.

    Dominion Paths

    • Bloodline Dominion: inherited realm and ancestral pressure.
    • Self-Forged Dominion: will-built, flexible, unstable.
    • Rift-Tainted Dominion: powerful, corrupted, identity-eroding.

    Guardian Pact

    • Agreement, not ownership.
    • Trust determines strength.
    • Origins: inherited, Dominion-bound, trial-formed.
    • Stages: vision, resonance, pact, manifestation.

    Beast Types

    • Loyal Guardian: traditional protector.
    • Forsaken Beast: abandoned, redeemable, volatile.
    • Unbound Beast: new legacy with no inherited script.
    • Riftborn Beast: rewritten guardian hunting former memory.

    Manifested Tools

    • Object form of soul pressure.
    • Weapon, artifact, interface, instrument, or utility form.
    • Can remember what the wielder forgets.
    • Should evolve visibly with Dominion and character arc.

    Canon Rule

    • Rank 10: controlled Dominion projection.
    • Rank 15: full Dominion infusion.
    • Rank 20: battlefield-as-Dominion.
    • First-year guardian visions are allowed; formal pact comes later.
